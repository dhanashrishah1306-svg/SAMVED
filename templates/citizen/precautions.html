ü¶† General Health Precautions

Hand Hygiene

Wash your hands frequently with soap and water for at least 20 seconds, especially after being in public places, before eating, and after coughing or sneezing.

  • Use hand sanitizer with at least 60% alcohol when soap isn't available
  • Avoid touching your face, especially eyes, nose, and mouth
  • Cover coughs and sneezes with tissues or your elbow

Healthy Lifestyle

Maintain a healthy lifestyle to strengthen your immune system and overall health.

  • Get 7-9 hours of quality sleep each night
  • Exercise regularly - at least 30 minutes of moderate activity daily
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains
  • Stay hydrated - drink at least 8 glasses of water daily
  • Manage stress through meditation, yoga, or relaxation techniques

Regular Health Checkups

Schedule regular health screenings and checkups to detect potential health issues early.

  • Annual physical examination
  • Blood pressure and cholesterol checks
  • Diabetes screening for those at risk
  • Age-appropriate cancer screenings
